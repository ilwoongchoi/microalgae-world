  Microalga are single-celled eukaryotic organism that live in water. They are often photosynthetic, which means they can produce their own energy source from sunlight and carbon dioxide.

Over the world, there are thousands of species of different microalgae that inhabit different environment and they all have different requirements for survival.

However, they all require abundant nitrogen and phosphorus for growth.

They can produce useful substances for human such as pigments, carbohydrate, lipid, protein and exopolysaccharides etc.

Throughout this website, I will introduce microalgae to you guys and share my knowledge of microalgae, so you can become more more familiar with them.
